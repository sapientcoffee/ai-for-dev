## The "Spark" (Core Idea)
We want to build an app that acts as a digital barista. It reads your mood, serves up the perfect coffee recommendation, and gives you a little nugget of coffee wisdom to start your day right. 

---

## ğŸ­ Feature 1: The Mood-to-Brew Matchmaker
*How are you feeling, and what should you be drinking?*

**Initial Thoughts:**
- User selects their current state of mind (Tired, Stressed, Happy).
- The app instantly recommends a beverage perfectly suited for that mood.

**"Double Shot" Ideas (Future Iterations):**
- **Expand the Moods:** Add states like "Creative" (pour-over), "Hangover" (cold brew with an extra shot), "Cozy" (mocha), or "Adventurous" (nitro cold brew).
- **Time of Day Context:** "Tired" at 8 AM = Triple shot Americano. "Tired" at 8 PM = Decaf Latte or Herbal Tea. 
- **The "Surprise Me" Button:** For the truly undecided.
- **Customizable Beans:** Ask the user if they prefer light, medium, or dark roasts before recommending.

## ğŸ’¡ Feature 2: The "Daily Grind" Tip API
*A 'Coffee Tip of the Day' API endpoint displayed on the homepage.*

**Initial Thoughts:**
- A simple, random tip about coffee brewing or trivia on the homepage.

**"Double Shot" Ideas (Future Iterations):**
- **Category Tags:** Tips categorized by "Brewing Methods" (e.g., French Press ratios), "Trivia" (e.g., origin stories), or "Fun Facts".
- **Dynamic Content:** A public REST API (`/api/tips/daily`) that other devs can use to get coffee tips.
- **"Save for Later":** Allow users to favorite tips they want to try at home.

## ğŸ¨ Vibe & Aesthetics (The "Cafe" Feel)
- **Color Palette:** Warm, earthy tones. Think roasted chestnut, creamy latte foam, and espresso black.
